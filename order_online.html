Mudraye

This product takes in speech through mic as input in their native language. It then converts the regional language to english. After that, corresponding sign language gestures are mapped to the words which are then displayed through a dot matrix display module. So basically, what is spoken in the native gets converted to sign language which helps people who are heavily depended on regional languages only to learn the sign language and enables the deaf to communicate efficiently.
