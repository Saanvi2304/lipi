ABOUT MUDRAYEIN

The model described provides a transformative solution for deaf and mute individuals by bridging communication barriers. It operates through a user-friendly interface where sentences are inputted via microphone or text.

Initially, the content is translated into English, ensuring accurate comprehension. Subsequently, the model converts the English text into sign language, a pivotal step in facilitating communication for the deaf community. The converted sign language output is then displayed on a screen, making it accessible to users.

This innovation significantly enhances accessibility, inclusivity, and education for individuals with hearing or speaking impairments. Particularly impactful in rural areas with limited access to sign language interpreters, this model offers a lifeline for those seeking effective communication. Its reach extends to children, aiding their learning of sign language and fostering engagement with peers and educators. By leveraging technology, this model not only breaks down communication barriers but also promotes equality and inclusion, empowering individuals with hearing or speaking impairments to participate fully in society.
